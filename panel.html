Steps to reproduce.

  1. Disable third-party cookies in chrome://settings/cookies (or disable just for incognito windows and enable and use this extension while incognito).
  2. Inspect this panel and switch to the Network tab.
  3. Enter a URL in the below box which you have cookies for.
  4. Hit send to make a simple GET request at the URL.
  5. Check the Network panel you opened previously, find the request, and look under the "Cookies" tab. Look at each cookie sent and blocked.
